Hello! I’m Jon, a fourth year medical student in the Medical Scientist Training Program at the Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine, where I received my PhD in Biomedical Engineering. My research focus is on the development, clinical implementation, and evaluation of custom-built generative AI tools for improvement of healthcare delivery. This work was the first to demonstrate clinical benefits of generative AI tools in radiology, for which I was named to the 2026 Forbes 30 Under 30 list in Healthcare.
