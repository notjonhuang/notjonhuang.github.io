[Open Ridemapper →](/ridemapper/)

Ridemapper turns rides recorded with a 360° camera and a GPS bike computer into an explorable map: a minimal globe holds each ride's trace, and clicking a gold stretch of route drops you into stabilized 360° video synced to the exact moment of the ride — drag to look anywhere, scrub along the path, or step through street-view-style photo stops.

Everything runs client-side in the browser; video is windowed out of equirectangular footage on the GPU.
